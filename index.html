Theses

Master's thesis
Title: A functorial rectification of finitely cocomplete ∞-categories
Supervisor: Denis-Charles Cisinski
A result, originally by Karol Szumiło, states that the homotopy theories of finitely cocomplete ∞-categories and of cofibration categories are equivalent. Using the theory of higher categories, I gave an alternative proof of this result.
Bachelor's thesis
Title: Locally constant sheaves with values in higher groupoids
Supervisor: Denis-Charles Cisinski
Following §7.1 of Jacob Lurie's "Higher Topos Theory", I prove that the sheaf ∞-category of a paracompact space X is the localization of an appropriate simplicial category of spaces over X.
