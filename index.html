Theses

Master's thesis
Title: A functorial rectification of finitely cocomplete ∞‑categories
Supervisor: Denis-Charles Cisinski
A result, originally by Karol Szumiło, states that the homotopy theory of finitely cocomplete ∞‑categories is equivalent to that of cofibration categories. Using methods from higher category theory, I gave an alternative proof of this result. My thesis can be found here.
Bachelor's thesis
Title: Locally constant sheaves with values in higher groupoids
Supervisor: Denis-Charles Cisinski
Following "Higher Topos Theory" by Jacob Lurie, I exhibit the sheaf ∞‑category of a paracompact space X to be a homotopy theory of spaces over X. For this, I constructed a relative version of the geometric realization–singular complex adjunction, equipped the relevant categories with model structures, and showed that the unit of the adjunction corresponds to sheafification.
